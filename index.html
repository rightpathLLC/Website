Why Hire a Public Adjuster?

The insurance company has adjusters working for its interests. A Public Adjuster works for the policyholder.

Claim Strategy

Policy Review

FAQ

Do you handle commercial insurance claims?

Yes. We assist with commercial property damage, equipment-related losses, business interruption, inventory damage, and other claim-related issues.

Can you review a claim that already received payment?

Often, yes. Depending on the facts, documentation, policy, and timing, a supplemental claim or further review may be possible.

Do Public Adjusters charge a fee?

Yes. Public Adjusters typically charge a percentage of the claim recovery. The consultation is free, and the fee structure is explained before engagement.

When should I contact a Public Adjuster?

Ideally before filing the claim or before the insurance company’s inspection. You may also contact us after receiving a low estimate, denial, or confusing claim decision.
